# Your Feedback Map

Map your actual feedback themes to the frameworks that will help you improve.

---

## How to Use This

1. **List your feedback themes** below (from reviews, 1:1s, peer feedback)
2. **Map each theme** to the relevant module(s)
3. **Prioritize** based on frequency and impact
4. **Start with your top 2-3 themes**

---

**Modules:** `03-Ownership-Execution/`

## Example Mapping

### Theme: "Sometimes provides too much context before answering"
**Frequency:** High (mentioned in last 3 reviews)
**Impact:** High (slows down decision-making)
**Mapped to:** `01-Communication-Clarity/01-Answer-First.md`
**Action plan:**
- Before every meeting, ask: "What's the exact question?"
- Practice: Answer in first sentence, then provide context
- Self-grade after each communication
- Track improvement weekly
